      Privacy Policy
      Last updated: July 2026

      This Privacy Policy describes how matherinth.github.io ("we", "our", or "us") handles user information when you use our website and authenticate via Google OAuth.

      Information We Collect: When you log in using Google OAuth, we request access to your basic Google profile information (such as your name and profile picture). We only access this information to display your profile within the application interface.

      How We Use Information: The retrieved information is used solely to personalize your user experience on this static website. We do not store, save, or transmit your personal data to any external servers. All authentication data is processed locally within your web browser.

      Data Sharing and Disclosure: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is never shared with any external entities.

      Data Retention: Because this website runs entirely in your browser without a database backend, we do not retain or store any of your personal data on our servers.

      Third-Party Services: This website is hosted on GitHub Pages and uses Google OAuth for authentication. Please review the respective privacy policies of GitHub and Google for information regarding their data handling practices.

      Contact Us: If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our repository contact details.
